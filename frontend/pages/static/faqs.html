Frequently Asked Questions

Home / FAQs

Shaghlni is a job platform that connects talented professionals with the companies that need them. We offer tools for job seekers to find and apply for jobs, and for employers to post jobs and find the best candidates.

Shaghlni is free for job seekers. You can create a profile, upload your resume, search and apply for jobs at no cost. Employers have access to free and premium plans depending on their hiring needs.

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and choose whether you're a candidate or an employer. Fill in your basic details, verify your email, and your account will be ready to use immediately.

Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page and enter your registered email. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours.

Find a job you're interested in and click the "Apply Now" button on the job detail page. You'll be prompted to sign in or create an account, then you can submit your application with your resume and cover letter.

Yes! You can apply to as many jobs as you like. Your profile and resume will be attached to each application automatically, though you can customize your cover letter for each role.

Your dashboard has an "Applied Jobs" section that shows all your applications and their current status — Pending, Under Review, Interview, Accepted, or Rejected.

Yes, you can create job alerts based on your preferred keywords, location, and job type. You'll receive email notifications whenever new matching jobs are posted.

Sign in to your employer account and click "Post a Job" in your dashboard. Fill in the job details, requirements, and salary range. Your job will go live immediately after submission.

Standard job listings stay active for 30 days. Premium listings are active for 60 days and receive additional exposure in search results and featured placements on the homepage.

Your employer dashboard has an "All Applications" section where you can view, filter, and manage all incoming applications. You can update the status of each application and contact candidates directly.

Yes, you can edit or delete any active job posting from your "My Jobs" dashboard. Changes take effect immediately. Deleting a job will close it to new applications but preserve existing ones.
